# Account & Login

**doc_id: KB-001**

## How to Reset Your Password

1. Navigate to the login page at https://app.example.com/login
2. Click **"Forgot Password?"** below the sign-in button.
3. Enter the email address associated with your account.
4. Check your inbox for a password reset email (may take up to 5 minutes).
5. Click the link in the email — it expires in **60 minutes**.
6. Enter your new password (minimum 8 characters, must include 1 uppercase and 1 number).
7. Click **"Save New Password"** and log in normally.

## Account Locked

If you enter the wrong password 5 times, your account is locked for **30 minutes** automatically. You may also request an early unlock via support.

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

- 2FA can be enabled under **Settings → Security → Enable 2FA**.
- Supported methods: Authenticator app (TOTP), SMS.
- If you lose your 2FA device, use a backup code or contact support to disable 2FA with identity verification.

## Changing Your Email Address

To change the email linked to your account:
1. Go to **Settings → Profile → Email**.
2. Enter the new email and confirm it.
3. A verification link will be sent to the new address. Click it within 24 hours.
