# Refund Policy

**doc_id: KB-008**

## Overview

We offer a **14-day money-back guarantee** for new subscriptions. After 14 days, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis.

## Eligibility

You are eligible for a full refund if:
- You request it within **14 days of your initial purchase**.
- You have not exceeded 1,000 API calls or used premium features extensively.

## How to Request a Refund

1. Contact support via the chat widget or submit a ticket.
2. Provide your account email and reason for the refund.
3. Our billing team will review and process within **5–7 business days**.
4. Refunds are issued to the original payment method.

## Partial Refunds

Partial refunds (prorated) may be offered for:
- Downgrade scenarios mid-billing-cycle (subject to review)
- Service outages exceeding 4 hours in a calendar month (SLA credit)

## Non-Refundable Items

- Setup fees for Enterprise plans
- Add-on purchases (e.g., extra storage, dedicated support)
- Charges more than 60 days old

## SLA Credits

Enterprise customers with a signed SLA agreement are entitled to service credits for uptime breaches:
- 99.9% target: Credit if uptime < 99.5% in a month
- Credit = 10% of monthly fee per hour of excess downtime (capped at 30%)

## Contact

For refund requests, please create a support ticket with subject "Refund Request - [Your Account Email]" and our billing team will respond within 1 business day.
